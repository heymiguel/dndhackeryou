Calais | Class: Bard

Calais loves a good story - and his favourite story to tell is his own. He always gravitated to the more intellectual bardic activities - telling the stories of great old heroes, discovering ancient lore, acting ancient stores written in archaic prose - than the more acrobatic activities. Now, just entering maturity, Calais burns with the desire to create new stories of his own daring - and not tell the old stories of old heroes.

Robin

Florian | Class: Warlock

Florian, a former cleric, once dedicated his life to his faith. In service of his appointment he never took a wife, and had no children. He dedicated his life to his community and to his god. Tragically, during a service at home ministry, a blaze swept through the temple. The fire consumed many. Among the dead were his parents and sister. All Florian loved, burned to ash and sinew in an instant. Florian suspects that the fire was no accident, and now scorns his god for allowing such an event to happen. He vows to find who or what took everything from him. He was once driven by faith, now he is driven by fire.

Phil

Xavros | Class: Ranger

Xavros learned to fend for himself at a young age. In his early teens, his village was attacked by undead, its inhabitants slaughtered. Xavros escaped to the forest, where he learned to survive, track, and hunt. While in the wilderness he discovered an affinity for animals, and often brings a furred or winged companion with him on hunts. Though he feels more comfortable around animals than other humanoids, he is fiercely loyal to those who earn his trust.
