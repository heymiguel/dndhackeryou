JORDYN

Calais | Class: Bard

Calais loves a good story - and his favourite story to tell is his own. He always gravitated to the more intellectual bardic activities - telling the stories of great old heroes, discovering ancient lore, acting ancient stores written in archaic prose - than the more acrobatic activities. Now, just entering maturity, Calais burns with the desire to create new stories of his own daring - and not tell the old stories of old heroes.
